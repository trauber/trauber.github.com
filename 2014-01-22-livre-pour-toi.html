Livre pour toi

I'm working on a typescript of Marguerite Burnat--Provins's Livre pour toi in English translation. It was translated by Elizabeth Brereton Pratt, mother to a dear friend, Mary Silverstein, sometime in the 1910s or 20s.

I'm writing it in markdown and using pandoc to convert the markdown to multiple formats.
